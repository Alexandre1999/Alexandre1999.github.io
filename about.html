About Me

I'm Ivan, born in Mauritius, i'm currently a second year Computer Science graduate student at the University of Paris XII.

I discovered programming quite late, i was nearly 14 when i wrote my first Hello World in .net. But this hello world was a true welcoming to me in the world of programming. The pleasure of solving problems and seeing your code compile is a satisfaction that i never got bored with.

I feel like my love for programming has also stemmed from my passion of envisioning, understanding and engineering. What would code do without the hardware anyway ? I worked on so much little electronic project by now such as making a bluetooth speaker and not mentioning my multiple custom mechanical keyboards.

I'm sure on two things about myself by now, i like technology and engineering. This has shaped me and lead to my current path. I aspire to reach the top of what's achievable in this field and will give all it takes for it. Never give up is my moto.

When i'm not working on codes you'll find me building keyboards or get out to visit some new places with my significant other.
